About me

My name is Dane Fournier, & I’ve been pursuing game development for the majority of my life. In that time, I’ve built prototypes for games in Unity, Gamemaker, & Godot, with the most experience in Unity.


My first steps towards game development were learning how to program. I learned the fundamentals of programming in a robotics class in middle school, learned the foundation of C# a year later attempting my first prototype in Unity, kept building small prototypes, then took a computer science class where I learned Java in my last year of high school, & finally formally learned Java, C++, & C# at Lonestar College.


Beyond programming I’ve also taken Graphic Design & Animation classes at the high school & college level respectively, as well as learning basic 3D modelling with Blender. I learned how to do all of these things because I wanted to understand at least some of the work that goes into every aspect of the creation of my favorite form of art.
